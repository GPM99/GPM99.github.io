About Us

Global Peace Ministries (GPM) is a non-profit registered society under societies act of India founded in 2004 by Dr. G. Joseph Chinababu at Hyderabad, India. The organization started with available resources within our financial capabilities. However, the need is not fulfilled the organization and appreciates to be part of the services to help needy through donations from volunteers. We participate and promote Health, Education, assistance to Orphanages and old age homes. Further, we also strive for women empowerment and prevent harassments against women by extending a helping to socially victimized women. We also organize various screening camps, skill development programs. We organize sapling plantation programs along with animal husbandry activities. We stand firm and walk together to extend a helping hand and to care for our brothers and sisters who are in dire need of help. We have been working towards empowerment women and children since 2004.Global Peace Ministries (GPM) extends its helping hand to everyone who is need equally irrespective of caste, creed, gender, race or religion. The GPM gives major emphasis on education for empowering women to earn a decent income through vocational skills. Legal aid and counseling for socially suppressed and abused women, education for underprivileged children by identifying benevolent sponsors, establishing a safe transit shelter for exploited girls/women. GPM also earnestly endeavors to establish associations and tie-ups with industrial and business enterprises under the scheme of corporate social responsibility (CSR) for providing hands-on training and employment for the trained women. Global Peace Ministries (GPM) pursue this disciple-making mission grounded in the identity, beliefs and values. Each of us do active participation in preaching and help people to learn about the Bible and God’s Kingdom. The Bible scriptures can help people to lead a peaceful life by acquiring knowledge and looking forward to God’s Kingdom. This Bible study course requires no commitment and is interactive, self-guided, and free of charge. Find out where we meet and how we worship.

Vision and Mission

Vision:

The founder’s vision is to establish peace and serenity globally. The founder Dr. G. Joseph Chinababu has personally seen and came across the struggles which are prevailing in the society. From his childhood he has a vision to eradicate these tribulation and struggles in everyone’s life. He made up his mind to eradicate these social huddles to the extent of his capabilities. Initially the organization started to serve the needy with own expenditure and reached possible numbers. The organization has started working on primary health care, education, technology training, skill development, animal husbandry, plantation of sapling programs, child and women empowerment, working for eradication of caste discrimination. We are extending a helping hand to Dalits, Muslims and socially suppressed people along with Gospel Ministry.

Mission:

  • The Mission of the Global Peace Ministries (GPM) is to minister to the spiritual, intellectual, physical, emotional, and environmental needs of all people by spreading peace, help for needy one, social services, Christ’s liberating gospel through word and endeavor.
  • The “Church” has a mission to fulfill in evangelizing the world with the "Gospel" of Lord.
  • Every person can have restored fellowship with God through 'Salvation'.
  • We promote Educational, Health, Welfare, Evangelism, Moral, Agriculture, Rural, Technology Training, Research & Development activities.

Objectives

  • To unite the people together for the development of physical, social, educational, cultural, moral, Intellectual and spiritual advancement and inspire them through ideas of spiritual, equality, love, help, support and service related.
  • To foster and develop programs with a view to promote physical, mental, moral, social, technical skills. We also give utmost emphasis for research & development along with spiritual advancement of students, working youth, business and professional people, rural youth, farmers, under privileged handicapped, widows and other children who are trapped in child labor etc.
  • To provide hostel or shelter facilities especially for students who are working in various fields. Orphans and old age people also get shelter in GPM.
  • To run school, technical institutions, libraries, reading rooms, journals & public related activities and other informative programs in the area.
  • To give help in hands to the people, without any distinction whatsoever, for self-help activities and such endeavors will be adopted to the development of people.
  • Ready to co-operate with any other Society or Association or Institution, having objectives altogether or in part like the objectives of this association.

The organization has a vision of healthy nation.

Primary Health Care
The organization has a vision of healthy nation. It cares individual health issues in rural and urban slum areas where the health care facilities are poor. GPM conducts medical camps and awareness programs. During the Corona pandemic, the organization distributed health kits like face masks, sanitizers, face shields and provided basic medical facilities to the economically backward people and for front-line medical assistants or warriors and to police, health workers and journalists. GPM also donated free food for needy people at hospitals and homeless people and ration i.e. rice, dal and oil for poor and needy.

Training and skill development with an eye on employment is the need of the hour.

Training and Skill Development Programs:
Training and skill development with an eye on employment is the need of the hour and GPM is a widely known NGO which is really working for skill development in India particularly AP and TS states since 2004. There is a growing realization that, having an educational degree does not always help in getting an appropriate job with one’s qualification. However, a minimum qualification of Class X combined with some vocational skills leads to employment opportunities. To expand the earning capability of the marginalized sections of society, by bringing them into the mainstream through the introduction of vocational skills to the needy. Our aim is to empower the underprivileged students/adults by providing sustainable livelihood leading to economic independence and keeping special attention to school dropouts with a minimum qualification of Class X. Knowledge of computers is a pre-requisite skill today for better performance in any job that helps in career growth. Since 2004, we provide computer training in Basics, Advanced Computer Applications, Tally, ERP courses and job-oriented courses etc. Course duration varies from 3 to 6 months. At the end of the training, they are examined and given Certificates. More than 90% our students pass with an ‘A’ Grade. Efforts are made to place the qualified students in Computer Operations jobs – such as Data Entry Operators, Accounts Assistants, Receptionists, Cash Memo Coordinators, Bank Executives and software/technical consultants etc.

Plant Husbandry:
The act or practice of cultivating crops and breeding and floating livestock; agriculture. The application of scientific principles to agriculture, particularly to animal breeding. It has been used to improve the quality of nutrition in products for humans and animals. The goals of plant breeding are to produce crop varieties that boast unique and superior qualities for a variety of agricultural applications. Plant breeding uses principles from a variety of sciences to improve the genetic potential of plants. The process involves combining parental plants to obtain the next generation with the best characteristics.
